About Our Group

We are a group of students who all attend Old Harbour High School, and we are currently in sixth form. As students at this level, we are constantly learning new skills that help us grow academically and prepare for future opportunities. For this assignment, we worked together to create a website that introduces our group and highlights the unique qualities of each member. Throughout the project, we shared ideas, supported each other, and combined our individual strengths to produce a well-organized and informative website. Being part of the same school and in sixth form has allowed us to collaborate effectively and communicate our ideas clearly. This project gave us the opportunity to strengthen our teamwork, creativity, and digital skills while completing an important assignment.
